Green ringer

Ringmodulator

Green Ringer





This is a ring modulation type effect.
A ringmodulater doesn't sound like anything else, but It has it's moments,
For example Hendrix used it on parts on purple haze and all along the watchtower
It sounds like a octave pedal when soloing and like a robot with chords.

This is my take on the classic circuit with added diode switching for 2 diffrent sounds:

-Germanium offers more vintage type sound
-Silicon for a more modern sound








NOTE! Pedal only works with 9v center positive power supply,
no batteri option availble at this time.
